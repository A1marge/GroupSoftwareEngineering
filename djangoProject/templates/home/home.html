Sustainability Bingo

How does it work?

Sustainability bingo lets you help the environment while having fun! Complete various challenges to tick boxes off the bingo board and win leaf coins. These coins can be exchanged for seeds in the store to help you grow your very own virtual garden.

Challenges range from online quizzes to exploring campus and scanning QR codes. Learn more about the environment and how we can help it while growing your in-game garden! Good luck!

Sustainable Food

Creating a future where all food comes from sustainable sources is key. Currently, the UK imports most of its fresh produce from overseas, contributing to high carbon emissions and unsustainable practices.

Sustainable Cities and Communities

As urban populations grow, keeping cities livable is essential. This means ensuring green spaces, resilient infrastructure, and strong community support, along with sustainable food, water, and energy usage.
