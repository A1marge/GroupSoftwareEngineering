About Your Green Impact Score

Your Green Impact Score is a measure of how sustainably you are investing in companies that prioritize environmental responsibility. It is calculated by summing the products of each investment’s sustainability rating and the number of shares you hold.

Green Impact Score = Σ (Sustainability Rating × Number of Shares) for each investment

For example, if you have invested in two companies:

- Company A: Sustainability Rating 8.5, 10 shares → Impact = 8.5 × 10 = 85
- Company B: Sustainability Rating 7.0, 5 shares → Impact = 7.0 × 5 = 35

Your overall Green Impact Score would be 85 + 35 = 120.

A higher score indicates a stronger commitment to supporting sustainable businesses and a greener future.
